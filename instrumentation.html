Instrumentation & Automation - Energy Star

At Energy Star, we deliver cutting-edge industrial Instrumentation and Automation solutions tailored to meet the unique demands of modern industries across New Zealand. Our mission is to help businesses optimize performance, improve safety, and drive operational efficiency, ensuring sustainable growth and long-term reliability.

We specialize in comprehensive automation and control systems that provide operators with accurate, real-time insights and seamless process control. From advanced measurement instruments to fully integrated PLC, SCADA, and DCS solutions, our systems are designed to simplify operations, reduce human error, and enhance decision-making capabilities.
