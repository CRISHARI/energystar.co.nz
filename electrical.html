Electrical

At Auckland Electrical Solutions, we provide end-to-end electrical services across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors. With over 30 years of expertise and a team of skilled engineers, we combine technical excellence with reliable service. From cutting-edge automation to trusted home electrical work, we deliver solutions that are safe, efficient, and future-ready. Our commitment to safety, quality, and client satisfaction makes us the partner you can count on for every electrical need.

We specialize in all areas of industrial automation with vast experience in PLC, HMI, SCADA, and MES programming. From legacy systems to the latest innovations, we uphold System Integrator status with all leading automation brands, including Rockwell, Schneider, Siemens, Omron, Ignition, and AVEVA. Our team ensures your operations are smarter, faster, and more reliable.

Our IT/OT networking solutions seamlessly bridge the gap between information technology and operational technology. We design and implement robust, secure networks that ensure reliable data flow, optimized real-time control, and integration with modern IT infrastructure. With our expertise, your industrial systems remain connected, efficient, and secure.

For over 30 years, homeowners, landlords, and building managers have trusted us for safe, effective, and guaranteed residential electrical work. From everyday fixes to major renovations, we make life easy with great communication and professional service. We work closely with other trades to ensure smooth project delivery and long-term peace of mind.

Our audits provide a clear picture of your electrical infrastructure, identifying risks and opportunities for improvement. With advanced thermal imaging and detailed audit reports, we help you:

  • Detect faults early
  • Ensure compliance
  • Improve safety
  • Save on energy costs

We provide dependable electrical maintenance for the day-to-day needs of businesses across retail, manufacturing, IT, and offices. From lighting fit-outs to upgrading outdated circuits, we handle jobs of all sizes with precision and care. Our clients value our responsiveness, professionalism, and strong focus on workplace safety.
