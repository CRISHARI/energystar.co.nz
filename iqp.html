What is an IQP and Why Do You Need One?

Under the New Zealand Building Act 2004, certain specified systems in buildings must undergo regular inspection, maintenance, and reporting to ensure they are functioning properly. These systems — such as HVAC, fire safety, electrical, and automation systems — directly impact building safety and efficiency.

Only an Independently Qualified Person (IQP) registered with the local territorial authority is authorised to:

  • Inspect these systems
  • Provide Form 12A certification
  • Verify compliance to support a valid Building Warrant of Fitness (BWOF)

Failure to comply can lead to penalties, insurance issues, and safety risks. With Energy Star as your IQP partner, you gain the assurance that all obligations are met without hassle
