Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) Systems

Fresh Air Without Losing Heat

HRV systems are designed for today’s well-insulated homes, delivering a constant supply of clean outdoor air while capturing and reusing the warmth from the air being extracted. That means healthier air for your family without wasting the heat you’ve already paid for.

Our team provides expert design and installation of HRV systems that keep your home fresh, dry, and comfortable year-round. By reducing condensation and controlling moisture, these systems create a healthier living environment and improve overall energy efficiency – a real benefit during Canterbury’s colder months.

Seismic Design Expertise

Engineered for Compliance, Safety & Resilience

At Energy Star, we provide in-house seismic design expertise to ensure all commercial HVAC installations meet New Zealand’s stringent seismic compliance requirements. Led by our Chartered Professional Engineer (CPEng), the design process covers PS1 through to PS4 certification, ensuring full compliance with NZS 4219:2009 and Building Code standards.

Why Seismic Design Matters

Proper seismic design is essential for both safety and compliance. By incorporating cost-effective seismic restraints, we help clients avoid costly delays and non-compliance issues. Our solutions safeguard building occupants and assets by ensuring HVAC systems remain secure during seismic events, while also minimising downtime through robust designs that keep essential systems operational in the aftermath of an earthquake.

Comprehensive Seismic Solutions

We offer a complete suite of seismic services, including customised restraint design for ductwork, piping, and mechanical plant. Our team carries out detailed structural load analysis to calculate restraint forces that meet compliance standards, alongside precise attachment design to ensure all HVAC components are securely fixed to their supporting structures. Each project is tailored with site-specific compliance strategies based on regional seismic risk factors.

Working closely with builders, consultants, and main contractors, we deliver seismic HVAC solutions that are cost-effective, fully code-compliant, and structurally sound—providing long-term resilience and peace of mind for every project.

Regulatory Compliance

Every HVAC design and installation we deliver is developed to meet or exceed New Zealand’s stringent regulations, giving you peace of mind that your project is safe, compliant, and built to perform for the long term.
