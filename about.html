EXPLORE MORE

At Energy Star, we are more than service providers—we are solution creators. With over 12 years of expertise, we have built a reputation as a single point of contact for Instrumentation & Automation, HVAC (Heating, Ventilation & Air Conditioning), and Electrical Services.We are experts in providing reliable and future-ready instrumentation and automation solutions, designed to optimize processes, improve safety, and maximize efficiency.

From residential to large-scale industrial environments, Energy Star ensures HVAC systems that are reliable, compliant, and energy-efficient.

Our mission is simple: to create smarter, safer, and more sustainable solutions that empower industries, businesses, and homes to perform at their best.Whether you need a measurement point calibrated, process inefficiencies resolved, or a tailored automation system, our Instrumentation team ensures your operations run with confidence and transparency.

Our Services

  • Commissioning & Parametrisation
  • Skilled in commissioning and parameterisation of on-site instruments
  • Manual adjustments or digital configuration (HART, IO-Link, etc.)
  • Ensuring instruments are correctly set up for accurate, stable performance
  • Process Optimisation & Tuning
  • Identification and resolution of process instabilities
  • Fine-tuning control arrangements for greater efficiency
  • Enhancing consistency, reliability, and overall productivity
  • Instrumentation Calibrations
  • On-site and off-site calibration services
  • Compliance with NZS ISO/IEC 17025 standards
  • Traceability to international standards for accuracy and reliability
  • Accredited calibration for Temperature, Differential Pressure, Pressure & Vacuum
  • Expertise across measurements such as temperature, pressure, level/volume, pH, conductivity, and air quality
  • Data Acquisition & Monitoring Solutions
  • Once-off investigation services to diagnose issues
  • Portable data acquisition systems for field flexibility
  • Custom site SCADA and reporting systems tailored to operations
  • Flow measurement and monitoring for precise industrial performance

  • Detailed Mechanical Specifications – Clear, customised specifications for each project, ensuring optimal system functionality and performance.
  • Council Consent Documentation – Management of all council consent paperwork, ensuring compliance and seamless approvals.
  • PS1 & PS4 Documentation – Preparation of PS1 (design compliance) and PS4 (construction monitoring) certificates, meeting all regulations and standards.
  • Fresh Air Ventilation – Supplying clean, fresh air to indoor spaces, promoting health and comfort.
  • Exhaust Air Ventilation – Removing stale or contaminated air for improved indoor quality.
  • Carpark Ventilation – Customised solutions to circulate fresh air and remove harmful emissions.

  • Industrial – Power distribution, switchgear, control systems, and automation integration
  • Commercial – Office wiring, lighting, backup systems, and smart energy solutions
  • Residential – Complete wiring, lighting automation, and safety systems

  • Stairwell Pressurisation & Lobby Relief – Maintaining airflow and supporting fire safety requirements.
  • Commercial Kitchen Ventilation – Expert design for kitchen exhaust systems, removing heat, smoke, and odors.
  • Humidity Control Systems – Precise humidity management for comfort cooling and specialised processes.
